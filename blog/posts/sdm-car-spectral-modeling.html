SDM-CAR explained: spectral structure in spatial Bayesian modeling

Why this matters: modeling graph spectral structure can reduce misspecification error and improve spatial prediction.

March 23, 2026

Problem

Classical CAR models can underperform when spatial dependencies do not match their assumptions, causing bias and weaker predictive performance under misspecification.

Approach

SDM-CAR introduces spectral-density modulation over graph structure and uses collapsed variational inference with MCMC diagnostics to capture richer spatial patterns.

Result

The framework recovers complex graph spectral signals and improves robustness and predictive performance compared with classical CAR baselines.
