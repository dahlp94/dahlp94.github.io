Designing BayesPilot: from model training to deployment decisions

Why this matters: reliable model selection requires linking experiments, evaluation, and rollout decisions in one clear workflow.

March 25, 2026

Problem

Many ML projects stop at model metrics without clear decision logic for deployment. BayesPilot addresses this gap by combining training artifacts, evaluation results, and deployment criteria.

Approach

The system tracks model runs, compares performance and uncertainty, and surfaces decision-ready summaries for selecting candidates to promote.

Result

This structure improves reproducibility and makes deployment decisions easier to audit and repeat across iterations.
