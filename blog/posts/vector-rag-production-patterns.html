Building a production-style Vector RAG backend

Why this matters: useful LLM systems need fast retrieval, grounded outputs, and clear traceability to source content.

March 24, 2026

Problem

RAG prototypes often work in demos but fail under real usage due to latency, weak retrieval quality, and poor observability of where answers come from.

Approach

I built a FastAPI service with Supabase-backed vector search and integrated modern LLM APIs, with retrieval and response flows designed for grounded, citation-aware output.

Result

The backend supports low-latency semantic retrieval and improves answer relevance while preserving source traceability for production-style LLM applications.
